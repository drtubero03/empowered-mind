About Dr. Tubero

Hi, I'm Dr. Adrian Tubero.

I'm a psychologist and the founder of Empowered Mind. I help people move through patterns that feel deeply ingrained — especially when insight alone hasn't been enough.

This work goes beyond insight — it's experiential, helping you access and shift patterns where they actually live. A significant part of my work is with individuals navigating midlife — supporting them through identity shifts, relationship changes, and deeper questions of meaning.

Together, we don't just understand what's happening — we work to actually shift it.

The work

A Different Way of Working

This is intentional, depth-oriented work that goes to the root of what's keeping you stuck — so real change can happen. Rather than circling the same insight, we work directly with the parts of you that hold these patterns in place.

Scope

What I Work With

I specialize in short-term, high-impact work with adults who are:

  • Struggling with old emotional patterns
  • Facing anxiety, burnout, or life transitions
  • Emotionally intelligent and self-aware — but still stuck

While my primary offering is the 12-session CSRT package, I also provide:

  • Ongoing therapy
  • Periodic check-in sessions for returning clients

I do not offer:

  • Couples counseling
  • Substance abuse treatment
  • Crisis intervention
While I don't offer couples therapy, I do work deeply with relationship dynamics — including attachment wounds, betrayal, disconnection, and trust repair.

Frequently Asked

CSRT is a focused, experiential approach to therapy developed to help individuals access and shift deeply held emotional patterns. Unlike traditional talk therapy, CSRT goes beyond insight — working at the level of felt experience to create real change.

This work is for self-aware, motivated adults — often in midlife — who feel stuck despite having done significant personal work. It's particularly suited for those who have plateaued in other forms of therapy or who want a focused, time-limited process with clear goals.
