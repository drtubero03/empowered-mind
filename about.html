About Dr. Adrian Tubero

Psychologist. Founder of Empowered Mind. Psy.D.

Bio

A practice built around real change.

Dr. Adrian Tubero is a licensed psychologist and the founder of Empowered Mind, a private practice dedicated to focused, experiential therapy for high-functioning adults.

With specialized training in experiential and depth-oriented approaches, Dr. Tubero developed Core Self Reclamation Therapy (CSRT) to address a gap she observed in traditional therapy: clients who had significant insight but remained stuck in the same patterns. Her work centers on helping people access change where it actually lives — not just in understanding, but in felt experience.

She works primarily with adults in midlife navigating identity shifts, relational changes, and deeper questions of meaning and direction.

Dr. Tubero holds her Psy.D. and brings both clinical rigor and deep compassion to each client relationship. Her approach is direct, warm, and deeply committed to lasting change — not endless process.
