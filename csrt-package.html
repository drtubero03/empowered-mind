Overview

Program Overview

The CSRT package is a 12-session, private, one-on-one therapy process for individuals who want to address specific emotional goals. Sessions are typically scheduled weekly or every other week, depending on the pace that best supports integration.

Focus Areas

What We Can Work Through Together

Emotional exhaustion or burnout
Chronic anxiety with no clear source
Repeating emotional or relational patterns
Grief that won't move
A pervasive sense of being stuck, even after past therapy
Lingering effects of early-life trauma or abuse
Recovery from emotionally unavailable or narcissistic relationships
Identity confusion or loss of self in midlife
