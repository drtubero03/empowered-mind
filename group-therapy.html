Group Therapy

For Women in Perimenopause

A small, focused group for women navigating the emotional, relational, and trauma-resurfacing terrain of midlife — held by a clinician who has lived it.

Apply for the Group

Why this group exists

Perimenopause isn't only physical.

For many women, midlife arrives with a quiet, unmistakable shift. The strategies that once worked stop fitting. Reactivity rises. Bandwidth shrinks. Old wounds — long held quiet by capacity, achievement, or accommodation — begin to surface with new urgency.

This is not a breakdown. It is a transition. And it is rarely talked about with the depth it requires.

This group offers what the medical conversation typically doesn't: a space to work with the emotional, psychological, and relational dimensions of perimenopause — and the trauma it brings forward — alongside other women doing the same work.

What we address together

The terrain of this stage

Emotional reactivity that feels unfamiliar
Trauma resurfacing as your nervous system shifts
Identity questions and a re-evaluation of life
Relational tension — partner, family, work
Loss of tolerance for what no longer fits
Grief, disorientation, and the sense of being adrift
Anxiety, sleep disruption, and emotional rawness
Reclaiming voice, needs, and direction

How it works

Format & structure

Small & intentional

6 to 8 women. Closed cohort — the same group meets each week so trust deepens and the work can go where it needs to go.

10-week arc

Weekly 90-minute sessions over 10 weeks. Each session pairs grounded education with experiential, depth-oriented process work.

Live video

Held over secure video so women across the New York metro region (and beyond) can join. Show up from where you actually live.

Trauma-informed throughout

Everything is paced for nervous-system safety. The work goes deep, but never beyond what your system is ready for.

What to expect

The shape of a session

Each meeting opens with a brief grounding practice and a check-in. From there, we move into the focus of the week — sometimes a specific theme (the inner critic, attachment patterns surfacing, grief, anger, the shifting body), sometimes following what's most alive in the group.

Time is held for both group dialogue and individual experiential work. You will be witnessed. You will be invited — not pushed. You will leave with something to sit with, not a checklist to complete.

Fit

Who this group is for

This is for you if you are:

  • A woman in perimenopause or early menopause
  • Feeling the emotional and psychological weight of this stage, not just the physical symptoms
  • Noticing old patterns, wounds, or trauma resurfacing
  • Self-aware and ready to work — but tired of doing it alone
  • Looking for a small, held space with other women in this terrain

This is not the right fit if you are:

  • In acute crisis or need stabilization-level care
  • Looking for a medical / hormonal consultation
  • Seeking primarily symptom management or coping tools
  • Not in a place to commit to all 10 weeks
If you're unsure, apply anyway. We'll have a brief consultation to find what's actually right for you — group, individual CSRT, or a referral.
