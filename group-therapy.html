The Midlife Shift

A 6-week experiential group for women navigating the emotional, psychological, and relational terrain of midlife. Thursdays, May 15 – June 19.

Apply or Join Waitlist

Why this group exists

A quiet unraveling. A turning point.

Midlife is often described as a hormonal transition. For many women, it's something deeper — a quiet unraveling, a shift in identity, a sense that how you've been living or relating no longer fits.

At times, it can feel disorienting — like the version of you that carried you this far is loosening its grip, before something new has fully taken shape. Questions begin to surface — about desire, direction, and who you are becoming beneath the roles you've held for so long.

This group is designed to meet you in that space.

What this is

A guided space, not a fix.

This is not a traditional therapy group. It's a supportive, guided space to slow down, reflect, and begin making sense of what's shifting — emotionally, psychologically, and relationally — while opening to the possibility that this stage of life is not only about loss. It is also a turning point.

The focus is not on fixing you, but on helping you feel seen and understood, more grounded in your experience, and more connected to a sense of direction and possibility.

Who this is for

This group may be a good fit if you…

Feel an internal shift

Something within you is changing in a way you can't fully explain — a quieter unease, a louder inner world, a sense that what worked before doesn't quite fit anymore.

Notice recurring patterns

The same dynamics keep surfacing — in relationships, in self-worth, in how you respond to stress — and you're ready to look at them with more honesty and less self-judgment.

Feel disconnected

From who you've been, and even more so from who you are becoming. You sense there's a fuller version of yourself underneath, but it's not yet clear what it looks like.
